About This Tool

AI-Powered Mindshare Market Analyzer combines real-time prediction market data from Polymarket and Kalshi with AI sentiment analysis from Reddit discussions. Using Hugging Face DistilBERT, the system analyzes social media sentiment to provide data-driven insights on market trends and public opinion.

How to Use:

  • 1. Filter by Platform: Choose Polymarket, Kalshi, or view all markets together
  • 2. Filter by Category: Browse markets by Politics, Cryptocurrency, Technology, Sports, or other categories
  • 3. Click "AI Analysis": Get AI-powered sentiment analysis from Reddit discussions related to each market
  • 4. Click "Trade Now": Visit the original platform to place trades on markets that interest you
